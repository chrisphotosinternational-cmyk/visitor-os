# Code of Conduct

VISITOR-OS contributors are expected to work respectfully, clearly and professionally.

## Principles

- Be constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Respect privacy and confidential data.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence.
- Keep the project safe, lawful and maintainable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Sharing secrets, private data or credentials.
- Encouraging scraping of private or protected spaces.
- Adding hidden tracking or vendor lock-in without review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may reject contributions, remove access or block releases when behavior puts the project, users or data at risk.
