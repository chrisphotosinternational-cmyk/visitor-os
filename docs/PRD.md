# Product Requirements Document

## Vision

VISITOR-OS helps small businesses convert website visitors into qualified leads through a modular assistant, conversation tracking, and CRM foundations.

## V1 Scope

- External widget integration
- Basic conversation tracking
- Basic lead capture
- Admin access
- Configuration by site/activity
- CSV exports

## Out of Scope for V1

- Full chatbot intelligence
- Complete CRM
- Payments
- Bookings
- Complex automations
- Native mobile app
